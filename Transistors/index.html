The Birth of the Transistor

The first transistor is built. Transistors are smaller, cheaper, and more reliable than vacuum tubes, so your factories can create them faster.

First Computer

The first digital computer is built. For the first time, humans can harness the power of electrons to perform arithmetic.

Integrated Circuits

Integrated circuits combine transistors and other electrical components onto small microchips, increasing efficiency. Building computers with integrated circuits is cheaper than with transistors.

Neural Networks

Researchers create the first perceptron neural network to recognize patterns in inputs.

Negative Reports on Neural Networks

Researchers find that perceptrons cannot recognize the exclusive-or circuit, and that any non-trivial patterns would require larger neural networks than computers can handle. Interest in neural networks wane. Your research costs increase, and research lab output decreases.

Backpropagation

Backpropagation solves the biggest issues with neural networks, increasing training speed, particularly for multi-layer networks, and allowing neural networks to recognize the exclusive-or pattern properly. Deeper neural networks are now feasible, spurring research in machine learning applications in many areas, such as image processing. The "AI Winter" draws to a close, and research costs and research lab outputs return to normal.

Computer Mice

The invention of the computer mouse allows users to easily navigate graphical user interfaces. Research lab productivity increases.

Personal Computing

With the advent of mass-produced computers with grahical user interfaces and mice, computers are now widely available to the public. Populairty increases significantly.

Reeducation Available for Research

Automation is displacing many factory workers, causing public opinion to decline. To combat this issue, you consider diverting research efforts to reeducate the unemployed in order to increase the rate at which they find new jobs.

Welfare Available

With unemployment still being an issue despite reeducation efforts, you consider providing better welfare in the form of integrated circuits. Beware: choosing to provide wellfare is permanent, and will permanently reduce your integrated circuit production.
